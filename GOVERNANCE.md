# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-0990 is an Open Occupation Blueprint under the cloud-itonami
collective. It is maintained by cloud-itonami project leadership and the broader
open-source community.

## Decision Process

- **Bug fixes and documentation**: PRs are reviewed and merged by maintainers.
- **Minor enhancements** (deps updates, test improvements): Fast-tracked.
- **Major feature additions** or **scope expansions**: Discussed via issue before implementation.
- **Scope boundaries** (contractor vs. operator): Non-negotiable. PRs that
  attempt to blur the contractor/operator boundary will not be merged.

## Escalation

If you believe a decision violates the project's governance, open an issue
with the label `governance` and request maintainer review.

See CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md for community standards.
